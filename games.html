This is a vanilla Java Minecraft Server running under Amp.

To get here: standard stuff. In your Minecraft multi-player login, you can edit or add servers. Either of these SHOULD work: swanlan.org:25565 or 147.92.65.240:25565 IF neither work and your minecraft works fine on other servers, send an email to swanlan209@yahoo.ca

If there is no one playing on the server, the Minecraft server may be asleep. To wake it up try and log on, regardless of what the status is. You should receive a message saying something like: "Server is starting". Wait three to five minutes and you should be able to log in normally. At this time I am not using whitelisting or any other type of player filtering on the server. This is intended as a family friendly server. It is mostly unexplored with a low player count. Collaborative players are most welcome!

The story so far: It started as another vanilla seed so I could keep up with my son ... epic fail! I built an emergency shelter. Then a small village. I used Minecraft Atlas to find some nearby villages. Sudbury is a mining village with experiments in automation, as well as what you can do with NPC. Haven is a waystation. Lomand is a farming village, still under construction. There are some other experiments as well.

Be warned: NPC Raids do happen against villages. Since I did NOT build fortifications, etc bad things can and do happen.

More players are welcome! We do operate a 'whitelist'. Apply for access at swanlan209@yahoo.ca
