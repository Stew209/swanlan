  • Welcome to SwanLAN!

  • Technical Information

    This server is operating on a fiber-optic network, provided through Axia. Their information is at https://www.axia.com/      

    The server is running on Rocky-Linux. Their information can be found at https://www.rockylinux.org/      

    I started using Linux in 2000. With the experience of many Linux server editions including: Mandriva, Mageia, CentOS, Red Hat, Ubuntu, OpenSUSE and now Rocky-Linux this has been one of the fastest and satisfying setups I have encountered.

  • Site Services & Status

    28 Dec 2021
    The Red Hat change of direction for CentOS drove a series of changes for swanLAN. First was the necessity of finding a new stable Linux platform. When Rocky-Linux was announced with the same folks who started CentOS, it seemed like a no-brainer decision to me. Throw in the learning curve, some hardware upgrades and you have a prolonged 'down-time'. swanLAN was here and working, but just for me. I am still selecting and installing services. The basics appear to be running securely and efficiently.
    As always, questions, comments and requests to swanlan209@yahoo.ca

    25 May 2020
    To make a long story short I ran out of patience with ClearOS. It has some fine strengths - it is very easy to install and activate basic services however its unique user control and systems are designed to drive you to pay for their enterprise system. Not what I wanted for a friends and family server.

    This time I tried CentOS. I installed a basic server (ver 8.1), with GUI and that proved to be the necessary magic. So we are back.

    Net down time was less than a month. Actually it was about two weeks before all services were restored. I have some clean-up to do, but services including Minecraft are operating, With a bit of luck my users won't see a thing.

    25 December 2019
    The server went offline, no indication as to why. MY best guess at this point is that IT tried to reboot after some updates and ran into my instruction to shut down and NOT automatically reboot. AMP has been updated as has Minecraft. Overall performance has exceeded my expectations.

    18 October 2019
    Thank you to websmythe who was our Alpha tester for the website. My first blog is posted. Minecraft is also up and running. More in progress.

    18th September 2019
    The web-server was activated yesterday.

    Once the root web server is up the next service will be the Minecraft server. a Cyber security site/blog is being planned. Check the site 'Blog' for notes on service activation.

    We will NOT be providing 'file sharing services' so don't bother asking.
